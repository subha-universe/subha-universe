About Us

Subah in literal sense means Dawn. A Dawn of hope, happiness and empowerment! Subah has been formed with the basic idea of providing a conducive atmosphere for growth and sustainable development among the under privileged of the society.

Our mission is to reach out to the last man in the societal ladder and provide him help and support. This support is not only meant to help them but it also to empower them and make them self sufficient.

Activities

Through all these efforts, Subah is trying to bring out a major change in the life of those people who are still far away from the mainstream and lag behind. This is an effort to enable them to be independent in all forms so that they can enjoy the Subah with a more positive attitude.

Educational Support

Subah has started hostels for daught hit region children and they are given free lodging and boarding. We also help them in continuing their education.

Training Programs

We empower women by starting Self-Help groups, giving them income generation support and also help them with legal counseling.

Health Check-up Camps

Subah belives “Health is Wealth” so we organise health camps for people who do not have access to medical facilities and thus suffer from ill-health.

Environmental Awareness

We also works towards environment awareness, conserving and nurturing greenery through various green activities like tree plantation, awareness programs in schools and colleges, creating seed banks, making bio-degradable bags.

Vocational Training

SUBHA has undertaken 11 vocational training programmes under women empowerment drive through skill development initiative. The programmes were organized for the women of 10 Localities in and around Pune.

Farmers Awareness

We organise trainings for the farmers related soil conservation, water management.
